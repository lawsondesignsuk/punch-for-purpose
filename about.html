Our Charity Mission

About Punch For Purpose

Punch For Purpose exists to turn fight-night energy into meaningful support for local communities. Every training camp, ticket, and fundraiser is built to help participants grow while raising real money for charity causes that matter.

Charity First

Our model is built around fundraising impact from day one, with every fighter campaign connected to a real cause.

Community Impact

We create events that bring local supporters, fighters, and partners together to raise awareness and funds with purpose.

Long-Term Vision

Starting in Portsmouth, we aim to scale city by city while keeping the same standards, transparency, and charity focus.

The Founders

Meet Mikey McKinson & Lucas Ballingall

Behind the mission are two professionals focused on combining elite coaching with genuine community impact.

Founder

Mikey McKinson

Mikey brings elite-level ring IQ, technical precision, and the calm leadership needed to guide first-time fighters from uncertainty to confidence.

His approach is simple: build strong fundamentals, stay composed under pressure, and earn every step with consistency. For Mikey, this platform is about helping people discover a stronger version of themselves while raising meaningful support for charity.

Founder

Lucas Ballingall

Lucas leads performance, structure, and preparation. He focuses on turning nerves into momentum through smart training blocks, clear progression, and accountability.

His mission with Punch For Purpose is to make the fight journey accessible to beginners while still delivering a serious, professional experience from week one to fight night.
