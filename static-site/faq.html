Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about training, fundraising, safety, and fight night before you apply.

Do I need boxing experience?

No experience is required. Our 8-week programme is designed for complete beginners and built around safe progression.

Is it safe for first-time fighters?

Yes. Coaching is safety-first, sparring is controlled, and event-day medical support is in place to protect all participants.

How are opponents matched?

Fighters are matched by coach assessment, weight category, and readiness level to keep bouts fair and appropriate.

What if I am nervous about fight night?

That is completely normal. Your coaches prepare you physically and mentally with step-by-step confidence building throughout camp.

What if I struggle with fundraising?

You will receive practical support, message templates, and guidance so you can promote your page and build momentum.

What does the £50 registration include?

Your registration secures your place and includes onboarding support, starter gear guidance, and access to structured training.

Still Have Questions?

If you need help before registering, our team can walk you through training, fundraising, and event-day expectations.
