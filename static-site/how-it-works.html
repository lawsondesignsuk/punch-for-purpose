Common Questions

Quick FAQs

Do I need previous boxing experience?

No. The programme is structured for beginners with coach-led progression.

Is it safe for first-time fighters?

Yes. Training is safety-first, with controlled sparring and event-day medical support.

What if I feel nervous about fight night?

That is normal. Coaches prepare you physically and mentally with gradual confidence-building.

How does fundraising work?

You receive guidance, message templates, and support to run your campaign effectively.
